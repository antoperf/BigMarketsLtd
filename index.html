02 — Our Story

Forward,
always.

Three decades of building the infrastructure that keeps Europe's retailers stocked and competitive.

1992
The foundation
Started as a small electronics shop in Milan. One phone brand, one warehouse, and the conviction that mobile was about to change everything.
1998
Mobile enters the picture
Pivoted fully to mobile phone distribution. Signed first major carrier partnerships and expanded warehouse capacity across Northern Italy.
2017
The refurbished play
Launched a certified pre-owned division. Gave retailers access to Grade-A refurbished inventory with full warranty backing and competitive margins.
2021
Full scale, full catalog
Now distributing 20+ brands to retailers across Europe. From flagships to entry-level, new to refurbished — every segment, every price point.
